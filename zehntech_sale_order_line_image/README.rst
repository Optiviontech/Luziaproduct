====================================
Sale Order Line Image
====================================

Enhance your Odoo sales and invoicing workflow by displaying product images directly on sales order lines, quotations, and invoices with Sale Order Line Image Odoo App. Sale Order Line Image Odoo module improves clarity and professionalism in your documents, helping to reduce errors and build customer trust.

Overview
========
The **Sale Order Line Image** module enhances the Odoo Sales and Invoicing workflow 
by displaying product images in **Sales Orders**, **Quotations**, and **Invoices**.  
Images are automatically pulled from the product record, helping customers identify products 
more clearly and improving the professional look of business documents.

Key Features
============
- Display product images in Sales Order, Quotation, and Invoice lines.
- Show images in PDF reports (Quotations, Sales Orders, and Invoices).
- Enable or Disable Image Display Easily from Sales Settings
- Keep Layouts Clean with Default Placeholders for Missing Images
- Support Images for Both Products and Product Variants

Installation
============
1. Download or clone this module into your Odoo **addons** folder.
2. Restart your Odoo server.
3. Activate **Developer Mode** in Odoo (optional but recommended).
4. Go to **Apps → Update Apps List**.
5. Search for **Sale Order Line Image** and click **Install**.
6. After installation, go to **Settings → Sales → Product Catalog** to configure the options.

Configuration
=============
1. Go to **Settings → Sales → Product Catalog**.
2. Enable the following checkboxes:
   - *Show Product Image in Sales Report*
   - *Show Product Image in Invoice Report*
3. Save the settings.
4. Product images will now appear automatically in Sales Orders, Quotations, and Invoices (including PDF reports).

Usage
=====
1. Create a **Quotation**, **Sales Order**, or **Invoice**.
2. Add a product that has an image assigned.
3. The product image will appear in the order line, invoice line, and in the related PDF reports.


Compatibility
=============
- Dependencies: **sale**, **account**, **product**
- Fully compatible with Odoo’s default reporting engine and workflows.
